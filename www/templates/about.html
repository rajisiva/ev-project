The Care Circle, our mission is to bring happiness to all those who have lost their family and hope in their lives under various circumstances. This project focuses on creating a network of volunteers and donators not controlled by a central administration, but by a system similar to a cloud, where the resources from the donators reach the needy through the volunteers of the network. There are three branches in this network focusing on the

  • Helpless parents
  • Talented and orphaned children
  • Homeless people
    •

There are plenty of resources enough for everyone in this world, but not equally distributed, which seems to be impossible. This project would change the current scenario by finding and motivating people to share whatever they can. A mobile application is developed such that a donator can easily suggest or invite more donators/users. The important feature of the application is that it keeps track of every people to be served. This network will be like a Facebook for the people who have never faced a peaceful life before.
